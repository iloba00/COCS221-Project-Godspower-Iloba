Vacations

Jamaica

Jamaica has beautiful sandy beaches, reggae music, and a lively culture, so I'd love to travel there. It is an excellent location to go on vacation because of the dining options, the atmosphere, and its historic sites.

Rwanda

Rwanda's vast wildlife, particularly its mountain gorillas, is what draws me in. I would want to tour its amazing landscapes, particularly its national parks, and I find its history to be interesting.
